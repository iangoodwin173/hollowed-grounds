by 4 friends:

anywhere from simple pixel RPG games
to horrendous war crimes in a hostile nation on the brink of nuclear war.
And minecraft too, obviously.

The group focuses mainly on the live-streaming aspect of content creation,
but does also keep up with prerecorded content
through Tiktok and YouTube as well. Most of the members also post on personal accounts,
so there is always new content
for your favorite member.

Along the way, the boys picked up some regular gaming partners (some would say additional members)
in D0ctahD0sage and ArtyBeQuacken.
